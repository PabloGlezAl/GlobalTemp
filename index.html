Global Temperature

Representation of the surface temperature of the earth for each country from 1743 to 2013

  • Learn More

The World

World representation in 3D that allow you to see the temperature of each country in the world figure.
The representation only shows data from countries that have information for the year analyzed.


Principal Timeline [1743 - 2013]

Secondary Year:

World Rotation

Combining a click and drag movement above the figure you can rotate the world.

Graph

Clicking in a country allow you to see a bar chart showing +/-5 years around the primary year selected.

Country Name

Passing the mouse over a country shows the name of the country for identify where we are.

Primary Year Selection

With the slider you can choose the primary year for represent the temperature in the world figure.

Second Year Selection

With the spinbox you can choose the sencond year, which allow you to compare the temperature of two years.

Settings

If the spinbox value is 0, the temperature of primary year is represented. If a sencond year is selected, the figure shows a comparison. (Temp.Primary Year - Temp.Secondary Year).

Bar Chart

It represents the temperature's value of the primary year selected and +/-5 years around it from the country you clicked in the world figure. It has different types of interaction to facilitate comparison.





Bar Chart

Representation of the temperature of the country clicked in the world figure. It shows +/-5 years around primary year.

Graph Interaction

Passing the mouse over a bar:
- Change bar color.
- Show temperature's value.
- Represent white line for comparison.

Graph Information

With the chart you can see the trend of the temperature from the years selected as well as temperature peaks.
